Conference Summary

3rd Annual Principles and Techniques of Complex Spinal Reconstruction: A Hands-on Cadaveric Workshop is organized by Weill Cornell Medicine (WCM) and will be held during Mar 30 - 31, 2018 at Belfer Research Building, New York City, New York, United States of America.

Conference Description :
Lectures in this event will be held at Belfer Research Building, New York City, New York, United States of America and Hands-on Cadaveric workshop will be held at Weill Cornell Medical College - Dept. of Pathology & Laboratory Medicine, New York City, New York, United States of America.

This two-day course is part didactic, part hands-on workshop that reviews the most common complex spine techniques used in contemporary spine surgery. The intended audiences are practicing spine surgeons as well as senior neurosurgery and orthopedic surgery residents.

Additional details will be posted as soon as they are available.
